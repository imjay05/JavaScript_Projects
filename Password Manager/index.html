Password Manager

A password manager securely stores and manages your login credentials, making it easy to access accounts with strong, unique passwords for better online security. It also auto-fills login forms, reduces the risk of forgotten passwords, and protects against phishing attacks. Many support multi-device sync, two-factor authentication, and encrypted storage, enhancing both convenience and safety.
